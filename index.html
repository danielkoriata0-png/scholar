Citation Expertise

All Major Referencing
Styles Covered

I format every source meticulously — from in-text citations to full reference lists — so you never lose marks on formatting.

APA
American Psychological Association — 7th Edition
Standard in psychology, education, and social sciences. Author-date format with DOI requirements for digital sources.
PsychologyEducationSocial Sciences
MLA
Modern Language Association — 9th Edition
Preferred in humanities. Works Cited page with author-page in-text citations. Flexible for varied source types.
LiteratureArtsHumanities
Chicago
Chicago Manual of Style — 17th Edition
Two systems: Notes-Bibliography (humanities) and Author-Date (sciences). Commonly used in history and journalism.
HistoryJournalismPublishing
Harvard
Harvard Referencing System
Author-date style widely used in UK universities across many disciplines. Clean, consistent, and widely recognized.
UK AcademiaBusinessSciences
IEEE
Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers
Numbered citation system used in engineering, computer science, and technology fields. Brackets with reference list.
EngineeringComputingTechnology
Vancouver
Vancouver Referencing System (ICMJE)
Numeric system favored in biomedical and health sciences. Superscript numbers in text with numbered reference list.
MedicineNursingBiomedical
